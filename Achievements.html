ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Motivating the student’s community to “learn by doing” and help them to understand that the "field is lab".
  • Encouraged students to set up social enterprises and more than fourty PG students and eight M.Phil scholars have completed their research projects under her guidance.
  • Introduced development oriented papers such as Entrepreneurship Development, Tools and Techniques for Transformation, Peace Building Strategies and Displacement and Social Work Practice in the department.
  • Received funds  from UGC and SIDBI for two consecutive years to conduct National Seminar on Social Entrepreneurship,   from UNICEF to conduct  two research studies on Educational Situation of Children in two District and from  IFCU-Paris to conduct an action research on displacement.
  • Organised motivation and language skill programme for the first generation learners every year since 2002 at the initial stage of their entry into the college and then conducted personality development programme as a follow--up.
  • Conducted motivation and personality development programmes for the school going slum children and youth every year since 2003.
  • Organised street theatre training for MSW students every year since 2002 for ten days.
  •  Organized Inter Collegiate Cultural mega event called “Spica 08” in the year 2008 for two days and had the credit of getting 50 colleges participating in the events.
